Welcome to SL Phone HUD
A slice-of-life sim for Second Life.

The HUD tracks your character's 7 needs — Hunger, Thirst, Energy, Fun, Social, Hygiene, and Purpose. Needs decay over time while the HUD is worn. Keep them healthy to earn XP and unlock content.


Earn Lumens (✦) by working jobs, completing odd jobs, and selling on Flare. Spend them in Haul, Lumen Eats, Sip, and Wavelength.


Build 7 skills by using related objects in-world. Higher skills unlock new interactions, better jobs, and passive bonuses.

Lumens economy

Currency: Lumens (✦) · Starting balance ✦500


L$ Top-up rates:

L$250 → ✦50
L$500 → ✦150
L$1,000 → ✦350
L$2,500 → ✦1,000


Subscriptions (weekly):

Wavelength Premium — ✦25/week
Gym Membership — ✦30/week
Flare Verified — ✦20/week


Robbery: There's a small daily chance of being robbed in grey-area zones. Up to 30% of your balance can be stolen. Robbery survivor? Check Canvas → Legacy for the achievement.


Odd jobs: 2 slots per day, reset midnight SLT. Pay ranges from ✦35 to ✦55 per job.


Brand deals: Unlock at 500, 1k, 5k, 10k, and 50k Flare followers. Pays weekly automatically.
